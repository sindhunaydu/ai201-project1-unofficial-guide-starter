Credit and financial counseling


Many nonprofit organizations provide credit and financial counseling services to assist members of the community with managing finances. Counselors — also known as coaches — may be certified, some may specialize in specific areas, and their organizations may offer a variety of other social services. These professionals can help with budgeting, managing credit, paying off debt, preparing for homeownership, and more.
